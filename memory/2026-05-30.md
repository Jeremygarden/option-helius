## 核心系统架构

### 1. 跑路风险评分系统 (Run Risk Score)

**数据流**: 18个宏观指标 → Sigmoid连续评分 → 动量调整 → Regime加权 → 综合分数(0-100)

**18个指标** (权重从walk-forward v2):
- MOVE: 12.0% | M7 Concentration: 12.0% | HY OAS: 9.27%
- Gold/Copper: 8.80% | DXY: 7.97% | NAAIM Exposure: 7.52%
- Yield Curve: 7.13% | PE Gap: 5.63% | Fear & Greed: 5.28%
- ERP: 5.20% | Sectors Above 200DMA: 5.20% | CAPE: 2.0%
- VIX: 2.0% | SKEW: 2.0% | TREND: 2.0%
- AIAE: 2.0% | Put/Call Ratio: 2.0% | RSP/SPY: 2.0%

**Sigmoid评分**: 连续曲线, floor=5, ceiling=95, 中点/陡度按指标设定
- VIX反向: 低VIX=高危(complacency)
- PCR反向: 高PCR=恐惧=低危(contrarian signal)
- 双Sigmoid: RSP/SPY (宽度偏差U形危险)

**动量调整**: α=0.15, 放大/缩减基于趋势方向

**5种Regime**: bull_late / rate_shock / credit_stress / vol_spike / normal
- 优先级: credit_stress(1) > rate_shock(2) > vol_spike(3) > bull_late(4) > normal(5)
- 信心≥85%: 纯regime权重; <85%: soft blend `final = regime_w × conf + normal_w × (1-conf)`
- 当前(2026-05-25): `bull_late` at 95% confidence

**三大哨兵**: MOVE>120, HY OAS>450bps, Yield Curve<-30bps
- 2+触发 → 核警报横幅(NuclearAlertBanner)
- 接近阈值 → 进度条(green→yellow→orange→red)

### 2. 期权扫描引擎 (Options Scanner)

**两层架构**:
- Layer 1 (`options_scanner.py`): 方向分析 + IV环境 + 策略过滤
- Layer 2 (`strategy_scorer.py`): 6策略评分 + 合约筛选

**方向引擎** (direction score -100 to +100):
- 30% MA alignment (price/20MA/50MA)
- 25% 5-day momentum
- 20% RSI(14)
- 25% position vs 52-week range

**策略过滤规则**:
- 近阻力 → ❌ Bull Spread
- 近支撑 → ❌ Bear Spread / Sell Put
- IV Rank<25% → ❌ all sell-side
- IV Rank>70% → ❌ pure long
- Bullish(>+30) → ❌ Bear Spread
- Bearish(<-30) → ❌ Bull Spread / Sell Put
- Earnings within DTE → ❌ sell-side
- No direction + low IV → 观望

**6种策略**: sell_put, covered_call, bull_call_spread, bear_put_spread, iron_condor, strangle

**合约质量门槛**: Volume≥50, OI≥300, Bid-Ask≤mid×8%, DTE 7-45d

**数据源**: yfinance (real data)
**实测**: `get_direction('SPY')` → Bullish, score=58.3, price=$745.64

### 3. 情景测试引擎 (Scenario Stress Test)

9种预设场景 + 自定义:
- Greeks-based Taylor expansion
- 计算PnL for each scenario per position

### 4. 分层刷新系统

- 实时层(VIX, Price): 5min
- 日内层(Fear&Greed, Sectors): 4h
- 日度层(大部分): 24h
- 周度层(NAAIM, AIAE): 7d
- 月度层(CAPE, ERP): 30d
- Staleness badges: 🟢(fresh) 🟡(aging) 🟠(stale) 🔴(expired)

### 5. 用户系统 (Zero-login)

- localStorage-based个人配置
- 4 tabs: 偏好设置 / 自选股 / 通知管理 / 主题外观
- Discord推送通知 via notification_service.py
